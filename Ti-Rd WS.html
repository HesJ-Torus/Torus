The Torus Ti Road Winter Sport is a sporting geometry road frame, similar to the Torus Ti Road, with the option of adding mudguards and a panier rack. It's comfortable enough to ride all day, in any weather - it's ideal for winter training, audax, sportives, or just riding around the countryside whenever you like! In keeping with the Torus traditional styling, the Road Winter Sport has straight chainstays, external 1 1/8" head tube, threaded BB, external cables.
