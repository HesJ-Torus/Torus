The Steel 650B from Torus Bicycles is a modern classic.
A fusion of fillet-brazed butted steel yields a light weight, but durable, frame with that unmistakable steel ride quality.
The Steel 650B was designed in the UK as a versatile cross country trail bike.
Double-butted 520 cromoly is used throughout, to help keep the weight down.
The sliding dropouts allows the bike to be set up a single speed or geared.
It can be run rigid or with suspension forks - the choice is yours. But with 27.5 inch wheels and classic steel tubing, a smooth ride is guaranteed.

Material

Fillet-brazed double-butted 520 Cromoly tubing

Head tube

Traditional 38mm headtube for use with standard (1.125”) steerer tubes

Tubing

35mm down tube, 31.9 mm seat tube (27.2 mm seat post)
