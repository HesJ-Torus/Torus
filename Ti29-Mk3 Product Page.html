Ti29-Mk3

Hardtail Mountaine Bike Frame

Titanium Frame

Available for pre order The Mk3 Ti29er has evolved over the last 12 months - and the wait has been worth it!! It's still a 29er hardtail designed on a 100mm fork, and the long, low classic titanium frame is still there - but with a few notable improvements:-

Specification

  • The chainstay/BB area has been stiffened by using a machined yoke. This has 3 benefits - increased lateral stiffness, increased tyre/chainring clearance and shorter chainstays.
  • The seat stays have been extended in front of the seat tube to increase lateral stiffness.
  • the seat tube has a slight bend in it to allow the rear wheel to be closer to the cranks to liven up the back end even more.
  • the rear hub dropout spacing is boost 12x148mm to improve rear end stiffness and increase the chainring/tyre clearance bu using a 3mm wider chainline.

Specification

  • The BB is slightly lower to improve handling, but not so low as to pedal strike too easily!
  • Dropper ready - the Seat tube is now 35mm diameter on all frames to allow 31.6mm internal dropper compatibility,
  • Single chainring only for 1x drivetrain compatibility!
  • Logos are now etched on the down tube and the head tube - stealthy!
  • 17" 19" and 21" frames available
