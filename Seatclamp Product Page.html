Titanium Seat Clamp

Seat Clamp

A titanium cut-out seat clamp from Torus Bicycles. The Ti nut bar can rotate to allow sufficient clamping without the problem of bolt misalignment as the clamp closes on the seat tube!

Features

  • Ti-3Al-2.5V band and nut bar
  • Stainless Steel M6 bolt
  • Band height 13.5 mm flared to 18.5 mm at the front
  • Weight: 31.8 mm 26 g, 34.9 mm 28g
  • Keep bolt head and thread lubricated - anti-seize grease recommended!!
  • Max torque 6 Nm
