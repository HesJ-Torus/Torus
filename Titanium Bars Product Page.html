Ti Bar

Torus Titanium MTB Flat Handlebars are beautifully made - and perfect for 29ers! Now available in 680mm, 700mm and 730mm widths, these bars are wider than most flats and help to keep the height of the front end low. The Ti-3Al-2.5V tubing is custom drawn to produce a light weight bar with natural titanium spring, so they're extremely comfortable - but stiff enough to race!

Specification

  • Material: Grade 9 T-3Al-2.5V alloy
  • Clamp Size: 31.8mm
  • Width: 680mm, 700mm, 730mm
  • Geometry: 0" rise, 7 or 9 degrees of bend/sweep
  • Weight: 760mm ~295g
