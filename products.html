Torus RTC rims

Torus RTC rims are a Road Tubeless compatible clincher rim with a temperature resistant brake track. These rims have been tried and tested and provide a nice lightweight aero road wheel. Torus MTC 29er Asym Carbon MTB Rims

XC race and trail tough carbon rims. 27mm wide asymmetric rims use an offset spoke bed to improve lateral wheel stiffness. Light enough to race on and tough enough for trail thrashing. These carbon rims give much greater lateral stiffness compared to the best alloy rims - so they can be built with less spokes, and lighter spokes if required. Combined with the offset spoke bed the result is a lightweight wheel with significantly more lateral stiffness. So when you pedal they go!

Steel 650B Frame

The Steel 650B from Torus Bicycles is a modern classic. A fusion of fillet-brazed butted steel yields a light weight, but durable, frame with that unmistakable steel ride quality.
