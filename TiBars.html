SPECIFICATION

Torus Titanium MTB Flat Handlebars are beautifully made - and perfect for 29ers! Now available in 780mm width and can be cut to size, these bars are wider than most flats and help to keep the height of the front end low. The Ti-3Al-2.5V tubing is custom drawn to produce a light weight bar with natural titanium spring, so they're extremely comfortable - but stiff enough to race!

Material: Grade 9 T-3Al-2.5V alloy

Clamp Size: 31.8mm

Width: 780mm, can be cut down

Geometry: 0" rise, 7 or 9 degrees of bend/sweep

weight: 780mm ~295g
